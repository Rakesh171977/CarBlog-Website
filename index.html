Character And Identity

Published on Oct 23, 2021 10:10:00 AM

The French never follow. Nor do they lead. They just do their own thing... accompanied by a nonchalant shrug. This independent streak permeates everything they do. Food, football, fashion, fast cars. Their approach is always different. While at times this doesn’t work, or isn’t as competitive, the French always push the boundaries. As a result, fleshing out a niche is something they do best. Duster, Kwid, Triber and now Kiger, all of these Renaults have succeeded in India because they’ve offered something different.
